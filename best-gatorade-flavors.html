All the Gatorade Flavors, Ranked (Gatorlyte and Gatorade Energy Drinks, Too)

May 10, 2024
blog

The best Gatorade flavors are fruity, refreshing, and, yeah, a little bit salty, too. They should give you a boost of electrolytes to make you good at sports. Or, if you’re like us, they should rehydrate you in the hellish aftermath of a night of drinking alcohol and eating Taco Bell. Just a typical Tuesday for the Sporked team, really.

Gatorade comes in a lot of varieties and formats at this point. In our last taste test, we stuck with regular ol’ Gatorade Thirst Quencher. But hey, go big or go home, right? This time, we expanded into their other two major beverage lines: Gatorlyte (which is like Pedialyte) and Fast Twitch (aka Gatorade energy drinks). We weren’t able to hit all of the flavors of Gatorlyte and Fast Twitch—some are surprisingly hard to track down—but the competition was still fierce. Ultimately, one Gatorade flavor does reign supreme.

Fast Twitch Strawberry Watermelon AmazonWalmart

I’m going to be quick and as impartial as I can with these flavors of Fast Twitch Gatorade energy drink, which you will notice have mostly landed at the bottom of the list. The good? Gatorade energy drinks have 100% of your daily B vitamins, 0 sugar, and only 10 calories. They also don’t have carbonation, which some people definitely prefer! The bad? They taste far sweeter than most energy drinks. Strawberry Watermelon is the worst offender. It’s cloying. That being said, if you don’t like the taste of other energy drinks (Monster, Red Bull, etc.) because they’re carbonated and (often) contain a lot of sugar, these might just be for you. —Ariana Losch
