Squawkabilly (Japanese: イキリンコex Ikirinko ex) is a Colorless-type Basic Pokémon ex card. It was first released as part of the Paldea Evolved expansion.

Card text

Squawk and Seize
イキリテイク
 
Once during your first turn, you may discard your hand and draw 6 cards. You can't use more than 1 Squawk and Seize Ability during your turn.
Motivate
きあいをいれる		20
Attach up to 2 Basic Energy cards from your discard pile to 1 of your Benched Pokémon.

Release information

This card was included as a Regular card, a Full Art ultra rare card, and a special illustration rare card in the Paldea Evolved expansion, first released in the Japanese Snow Hazard expansion. The Regular and Full Art ultra rare prints feature artwork by PLANETA Mochizuki, while the special illustration rare print features artwork by Amelicart. The Regular print was reprinted, alongside a new Shiny ultra rare print with artwork by PLANETA Mochizuki, in the Shiny Treasure ex subset. Both the Regular print and the Shiny ultra rare print were included in the Paldean Fates expansion.

The Regular print is also reprinted in the Japanese Stellar Miracle Deck Build Box.

Trivia

  • The functionality of Squawk & Seize is similar to Dark Asset on Crobat V and Dedechange on Dedenne-GX.[1] It is a Basic Pokémon that allows the opponent to take two Prize cards when it is knocked out, and it draws cards via an Ability that is restricted to being used once per turn per player.

Origin

This card's Regular, ultra rare, and Shiny ultra rare prints feature Squawkabilly in its Green Plumage form, while the special illustration rare print features Squawkabilly in its Green, Blue, Yellow, and White Plumage forms.
