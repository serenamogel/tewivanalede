Angela Simmons | Career

Even before she became a prosperous entrepreneur, Angela was no stranger to the spotlight, having been born into a family of hip-hop moguls.

However, she experienced extraordinary fame after she began to participate in the reality television program “Run’s House,” which was produced by her family.

She took over as editor of the “Angela’s Rundown” mini-magazine in 2006, which was released with the “Word Up!” magazine.

She persisted with the publication until “Word Up!” ceased to be published in April 2012.

In 2007, Simmons and her older sister Vanessa started the footwear business “Pastry Footwear.”

She eventually made her business endeavors her top priority, but in 2009, she returned to reality television by participating in the “Daddy’s Girls” series with her sister.
