Meredith began her journalism career while still a student at The University of Georgia. Between May 2007 – Aug 2007, Mark worked as an intern for CNN’s The Situation Room. Furthermore, he also served as an intern for NBC News for 3 months. Mark interned for Nightly News, Today, Dateline.

Immediately after graduation, Meredith joined Myrtle Beach, South Carolina based WMBF in May 2009 where he served as a general assignment reporter until Dec 2011. Additionally, the senior reporter has worked as a white house correspondent and national correspondent for Nexstar Media Group, Inc. located in Washington D.C. Metro Area. Mark worked at this station from Sep 2015 – Apr 2019.
