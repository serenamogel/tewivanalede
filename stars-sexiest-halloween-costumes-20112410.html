February 03, 2024

Halloween gives stars a perfect excuse to dress in their sexiest attire.

From Kendall Jenner and Kylie Jenner dressing up in Sugar & Spice’s corset and underwear costumes from Batman Forever in 2023 to Ice Spice’s cheeky Betty Boop getup in 2023, stars are willing to bare all for the spooky holiday.

Ice Spice ditched her signature red locks for a black coiffure, perfectly imitating the animated character. She topped the look off with fishnet tights, a heart-shaped garter and long nails while performing at the iHeart Powerhouse 105.1 event in New Jersey.

In 2019, Demi Lovato made the Victorian-era look sexy with her Marie Antoinette inspired costume while Cardi B dressed as a naughty nurse with a uniform that commanded attention.
