What is the significance of the Lil in rappers’ names?

The word Lil, which is a contraction of the word little, originally arose in the 1800s. Tiger Webb, an ABC language expert (and rap enthusiast), explained, “It’s in the shape of a child’s nursery rhyme – Lil boy bear, Lil gal bear.” But, according to Webb, the way it’s being utilized in hip hop – to identify lineage — looks to be original.

What is Lil Jon’s claim to fame?

If you like hip-hop or even pop music, you’ve definitely heard of Jonathan Smith, better known by his stage name Lil Jon.
