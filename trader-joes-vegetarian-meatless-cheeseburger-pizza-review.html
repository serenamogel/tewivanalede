Trader Joes Vegetarian Meatless Cheeseburger Pizza Review

May 16, 2024
blog

Trader Joe’s Vegetarian Meatless Cheeseburger Pizza is the newest addition to their freezer section. This combo of two classic American foods sounds delicious, but is it? Does the burger overpower the pizza? Is it still too pizza-y? Can we condone pickles on a pizza? Let’s find out! We tasted it so you don’t have to buy it without knowing whether or not it’s good.

New Product!

Trader Joe’s Vegetarian Meatless Cheeseburger PizzaTrader Joe’s

We were excited but scared to try this one. Cheeseburger pizza sounds delicious, but we could see it easily going wrong. I’m thrilled to report, however, that we loved this.

It’s gooey, meatlessly meaty, and savory. I loved the brightness of the pickles. My only gripe with this pizza is that they can take off the flavorless tomato chunks. We dipped this in thousand island dressing and had an absolute ball. It was delicious.

Credit: Liv Averett / Trader Joe’s

Rating:

8.5/10
