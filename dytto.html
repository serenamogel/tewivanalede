Career

Before starting her career as a dancer, she was a gymnast and a cheerleader. She created her YouTube channel on 24 November 2014. In 2014, she uploaded her first video of “Barbie Trap Girl Remix,” which earned her much recognition. Talking about it, she says-

My mom filmed it, and it was very out of focus. It was really dark and in a parking garage of our apartment. I edited it in iMovies, so there are some pretty good cross dissolves in there.”

In 2016, she participated in the “World of Dance (WOD),” an international urban dance competition. There she performed her famous ‘Barbie Girl’ dance, which made her famous internationally and gained her a million followers.

She has more than 2 million subscribers on her YouTube Channel. She has worked in movies like “Life of the Party (2018),” “AXI: Avengers of Extreme Illusions (2011)” and “Monsterz (2015).” She made her acting debut with the film “Monsterz (2015).”
