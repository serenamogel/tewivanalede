The Best Grapefruit Soda We Found in a Taste Test

July 02, 2024
blog

The best grapefruit soda is light, citrusy, and refreshing. I wasn’t well-versed in grapefruit soda before this taste test, but now I’m a pro and a superfan.

Grapefruit soda is so delicious and we should all be drinking it more regularly. The sweet yet tart flavor mixed with carbonation is incredible. It’s great on its own or mixed into a cocktail (Paloma gang, stand up!). Whether you’re a grapefruit soda novice or a longtime fan, you should be buying these grapefruit soda brands when you head to the grocery store. Here we have  the best grapefruit soda for Palomas, the best diet grapefruit soda, and so much more.

Best Bottled

Izze Sparkling GrapefruitInstacart

You’ve seen Izze behind the counter at Chipotle, but have you actually tried it? You should. It’s good! It’s fizzy and light and not super sweet. The prominent flavor is citrus, so it makes sense that they stock it at America’s favorite burrito chain. That zesty brightness is perfect for cutting through fatty meats and rich dairy. This is the best grapefruit soda to pair with a fat burrito. You’ll be glad you did.

Best Real Sugar

Jarritos GrapefruitInstacart

Jarritos are good in general, so it’s no surprise that they make one of the best grapefruit soda options you can buy. In fact, this is the second-best Jarritos flavor of all their flavors. It’s also on the more mild side in terms of citrus and carbonation. If you can’t take a ton of flavor or bubbles, this is a good option for you. Heartburn, who???

Credit: Merc / Instacart

Rating:

8.5/10

Best Sugar Free

Fresca Original CitrusInstacart

I cannot believe I’m saying this, but I love a sugar free soda. That sugar free soda is Fresca. Fresca is so damn delicious! Somehow the citrus taste manages to mask any weird fake sugar flavor that plagues most sugar free sodas. Plus, it genuinely tastes like grapefruit and not just citrus. Don’t sleep on Fresca. Go buy some immediately! It’s not just the best diet grapefruit soda, it’s one of the best diet sodas, period.

Credit: Merc / Instacart

9/10

Best Italian

Whole Foods Organic Pink Grapefruit Italian SodaWhole Foods

I tried a few versions of “Italian” soda (aka soda made with natural flavors) and this version from Whole Foods is definitely the best grapefruit soda when it comes to Italian sodas. It’s also just one of the best grapefruit soda choices in general. It tastes wonderful. It’s sweeter than most of the other grapefruit sodas we tried. That being said, it still has a tart citrusy kick. Plus, the pretty packaging makes this feel fancy and perfect for sitting on a bar cart.

Credit: Merc / Whole Foods

Rating:

9.5/10

Best of the Best

SquirtInstacart

Squirt is the best grapefruit soda because it actually tastes like grapefruit. It’s bright and citrusy. It’s crisp and super carbonated. It’s super sweet. And while I’ve suggested using many of these for Palomas, the classic Paloma is literally made with Squirt, specifically. Squirt is a classic for a reason. It is the best grapefruit soda money can buy.
